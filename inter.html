Intermediate


When graduating, the majority of students focus on the intermediate. This is because Inter needs to study for a higher education or job in the future. That is why this is a crucial turning point for a career. Student life starts from here. That is why it is so important to decide which group to take on Inter. Students should take some time and increase their awareness before selecting a group that will guide them throughout their life.


Intermediate is a 2 years course which are taught at the Government or Private or Cooperative Junior colleges.
Intermediate is a General course which gives you the flexibility to do various graduation courses along with Engineering and Medical. Most of the schools and colleges accept all Class 10th passed students for enrolling in the Intermediate course. If you opt to do Intermediate after class 10th then you will have several career path to follow such as:

1.Medical
2.Non-medical (Engineering Degree)
3.Humanities
4.Commerce
5.Vocational Studies
