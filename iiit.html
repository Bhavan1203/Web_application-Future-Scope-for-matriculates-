Indian Institute of Information Technology


IIIT stands for Indian Institute of Information Technology. The main focus of these institutes is on courses related to Information Technology though they also offer few other courses. Ministry of Human Resource Development funds and manages these institutes. These institutes are classified as per the funded organizations. Institutes funded by Central Government, Institutes funded by Public-private partnership and institutes that finance individually are the categories.
The institutes in Allahabad, Jabalpur, Gwalior, Kancheepuram are funded by Central Government and these institutes attained the recognition of Institutes of National Importance. The institutes in Una, Sri City, Guwahati, Kota, Vadodara, Trichy and few others are funded in the partnership of State Governments and Industries.

What are the educational programs in IIIT?


  • Bachelor of Technology
  • Master of Technology
  • Master of Science
  • Dual degree Programs
  • Master of Business Administration in Information Technology
  • Ph.D.
