Industrial Training Institute


ITI courses after 10th are technical courses meant to give practical training and education in numerous industrial domains, including the IT sector. Students who have finished their 10th standard (or equivalent) from a recognized board or institution are eligible for these courses. ITI classes after the tenth grade offer a broad range of IT-related topics, such as web design, networking, programming languages, hardware upkeep, and software maintenance. The goal of these courses is to give students a practical and technical understanding of the most recent developments in IT trends and technology.
