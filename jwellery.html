Jewellery Designing


Jewellery design courses are suitable for candidates who want to learn how to design and manufacture Jewellery. Jewellery design courses are popular design specialization due to the immense growth of Jewellery manufacturing and its market in recent years.

Students can explore courses on Jewellery design online that are available both for beginners as well as experienced Jewellery designers. Udemy is one such platform that offers a variety of Jewellery design courses starting from INR 455. Udemy Jewellery Design Courses help students to learn Jewellery design concepts through animated videos or to learn the latest techniques in Jewellery designing. A Jewellery designer can expect to earn INR 3.4 - 3.5 lakhs annually as a fresher. Freshers can upskill and learn Adobe Photoshop, Computer-Aided Design (CAD), and 3D Rendering, as they are expected to offer an average compensation of INR 4.2 - 4.7 lakhs annually.
